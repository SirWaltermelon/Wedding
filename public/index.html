Our story or something like that

It all started with a text.

While both at a sleep over with friends around Christmas time in 2011 with people we mostly no longer talk to (hi people we don’t talk to, nice to see you’re doing well) one prank phone call led to another which then resulted in us exchanging numbers. Since that night we have never been out of contact for more than a few days.


After nearly 10 years of being together and experiencing the ups and downs of going through a relationship: graduating high school, college, first jobs and apartments, moving to a different state, moving BACK, buying a first home and our biggest test yet - getting a dog (see Yoshi) we decided it was time to seal the deal.


As neither of us are big on being in the spotlight, we always knew we would likely elope at a courthouse just the two of us and keep things simple. (Also can we talk about how insane weddings actually are? Why should WE throw a party for YOU to celebrate US? Okay- not the time but I really think we should evaluate this)


In the interest of not being totally hated by family and friends for not involving them in our day we had some pictures taken to capture and commemorate it.
